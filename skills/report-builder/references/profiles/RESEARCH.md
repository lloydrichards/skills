# Research profile

**Reader job:** Understand what trustworthy sources collectively support.

**Reasoning chain:** Question and scope → method and source selection → themes or competing positions → synthesis → gaps → implications.

Open with the research question and short synthesis. Group evidence by idea, not source order. Distinguish consensus, disagreement, inference, and missing evidence. Cite claims locally and describe why sources were selected. End with implications and what further evidence would change the conclusion.

Avoid annotated-bibliography narration, false consensus, and recommendations that outrun the research.
