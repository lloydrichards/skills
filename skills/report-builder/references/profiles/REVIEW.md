# Review profile

**Reader job:** Understand what was examined, what matters, and what to do.

**Reasoning chain:** Scope and criteria → findings → evidence → impact → recommendation.

Open with the overall outcome and review boundary. Organize findings by severity, concept, or affected behavior rather than discovery order. Each actionable finding should state the observed condition, why it matters, its evidence, and a concrete action. End with priorities, open checks, and what was not reviewed.

Avoid presenting suggestions as findings, hiding limitations, or treating all observations as equally important.
