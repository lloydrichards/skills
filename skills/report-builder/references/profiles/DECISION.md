# Decision profile

**Reader job:** Make or understand a consequential choice.

**Reasoning chain:** Context → drivers and constraints → viable options → trade-offs → recommendation or decision → consequences.

Open with the decision status and recommended choice when one exists. Compare options against explicit drivers rather than generic pros and cons. Preserve why rejected options lost. End with positive and negative consequences, open questions, and any review trigger or superseding condition.

Avoid option theater, unstated criteria, and recommendations that omit costs or reversibility.
